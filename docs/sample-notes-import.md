# Goblin

_Small Humanoid (Goblinoid), Neutral Evil_

**Armor Class** 15 (leather armor, shield)
**Hit Points** 7 (2d6)
**Speed** 30 ft.

## Stats

| STR    | DEX     | CON     | INT     | WIS    | CHA    |
| ------ | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------ | ------ |
| 8 (-1) | 14 (+2) | 10 (+0) | 10 (+0) | 8 (-1) | 8 (-1) |

## Skills

Stealth +6

## Senses

Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 9

## Languages

Common, Goblin

## Challenge

1/4 (50 XP)

---

## Abilities

### Nimble Escape

The goblin can take the Disengage or Hide action as a bonus action on each of its turns.

---

## Actions

### Scimitar

_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
_Hit:_ 5 (1d6 + 2) slashing damage.

### Shortbow

_Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +4 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target.
_Hit:_ 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.

---

## Tactics

Goblins prefer ambushes and hit-and-run tactics. They use their Nimble Escape to disengage and hide after attacking, making them frustrating opponents for melee-focused characters.

## Loot

- 1d6 silver pieces
- Scimitar (damaged)
- Shortbow with 10 arrows
